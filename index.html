MESSAGE FROM THE FOUNDER

"I am a mother, a wife, and above all, a lifelong learner dedicated to empowering rural tribal students through digital education. My mission is to bridge passionate learners with committed teachers across India. Over the past six years, despite many challenges, we have successfully supported numerous rural tribal learners. With over three decades of experience as an educationist, I have played leadership roles at some of India’s most respected institutions, including Rainbow International School, Mumbai, where I served as Chief Operating Officer, as well as the Delhi Public School (DPS) franchise and G.D. Goenka Public School. Today, as the driving force behind the UCatapult Foundation, I continue to blend my rich educational experience with my passion for social impact. If you believe in the transformative power of digital education and community-driven collaboration, I invite you to join our team! Together, we can expand this impactful initiative and create a brighter future for countless rural students nationwide"

- Prema Mishra

On August 15, 2025, in honor of Independence Day, the founder of UCatapult was invited as a guest of honor by the principal of Rashtramata Indira Gandhi Military School in Maharashtra, who was inspired by the organization's work.
